Centralized CBS

The Centralized Core Banking Solution (CBS) for Vyapari Service Co-operative Society is a cutting-edge, integrated digital banking platform designed to streamline the society’s financial operations.

Through Centralized CBS, members can enjoy faster and more reliable services such as deposits, withdrawals, loan processing, and account inquiries. The system enhances efficiency by automating routine tasks, reducing manual errors, and improving transparency in all financial activities. It also strengthens data security and ensures compliance with regulatory standards.

By bringing all banking functions under one centralized system, Vyapari Service Co-operative Society ensures a seamless, consistent, and convenient experience for its members—anytime, anywhere.



Features :

1. Web-based in cloud (multi-tier) / Windows thick client (C/S)

2. Anywhere, anytime access with Single Window Banking & Teller System

3. Scheduled and automated processes

4. Settlement account transfer automation

5. TDS and GST automation

6. User-friendly interface without compromising performance/security

7. Highly responsive, even for complex reports

8. Mobile app support for login, image capturing, etc.

9. Multifactor authentication

10. Maker-checker facility in all entry forms

11. Customer ID integration across modules

12. Multiple entries for addresses, names, phones, KYC docs

13. Parameterized system reducing customization requirements

14. Multi-image scanning for photos, signatures, documents

15. Facility to store images/documents in database
