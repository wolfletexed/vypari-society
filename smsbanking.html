SMS Banking System

Stay informed and connected with real-time SMS alerts from our SMS Banking system. This service ensures that customers receive instant updates on all major transactions and account activities, improving transparency, convenience, and security.

Deposit Alerts

1. An SMS notification will be sent for every deposit transaction posted to your account.

2. You will receive timely alerts regarding the upcoming maturity of your deposits.

3. A confirmation message will be issued whenever a deposit is renewed successfully.

4. Notifications will be sent when interest from your deposit is transferred to the designated account.

5. Alerts will be issued whenever a pledge or lien is created, updated, or removed on your deposit.

Fund Transfer & Cheque Alerts

1. You will receive SMS alerts for all fund transfers, including successful, pending, or failed transactions.

2. Real-time notifications will be provided regarding the status of cheque clearing, whether cleared, returned, or under process.

Loan Alerts

1. SMS alerts will be issued for all loan-related transactions recorded in your account.

2. You will receive advance reminders of loan instalment due dates for timely repayment.

3. Notifications will be sent prior to the maturity of your loan to keep you informed.

4. Overdue alerts will be issued in the event of missed or delayed instalments.

5. Messages will be sent regarding demand generation and recovery actions related to your loan.

Chitty / Kuri Alerts

1. SMS notifications will be sent for all chitty-related transactions associated with your membership.

2. Information will be communicated before and after chitty auctions with relevant updates.

3. You will receive reminders of chitty instalment due dates to ensure timely payments.

4. Alerts will be issued if any chitty instalment becomes overdue, prompting necessary follow-up.
