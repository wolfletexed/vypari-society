ABOUT US



A Journey Rooted in Trust, Grown Through Unity




Founded with a vision to uplift small traders and empower local entrepreneurs, Vyapari Service Cooperative Society began its journey in the bustling market lanes of Munnar in the early 1990s. What started as a collective of just a few dedicated vendors soon blossomed into a trusted institution—offering essential financial services, marketing assistance, and business mentorship. Today, the cooperative stands as a beacon of community growth, where members support one another in facing market challenges, accessing funding, and scaling their ventures. Vyapari isn’t just a society—it’s a living testament to what cooperation, integrity, and shared prosperity can achieve.
