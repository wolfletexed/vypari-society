Welcome to Devikulam
Vyapari Co-operative Society

Vyapari Society is a community of dedicated traders, entrepreneurs, and business owners located in the beautiful town of Adimali. Established with a vision to promote local businesses and create a supportive environment for entrepreneurs, our society has grown to become a hub for trade, commerce, and collaboration in the region.

Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to a Strong Network

Born from a shared vision of cooperation, the Devikulam Taluk Traders and Industrialists Service Cooperative Society began in 1997 under the leadership of the Adimali business community. What started as a small group of like-minded merchants soon became Kerala’s first cooperative society for traders, officially formed in 1998.

Created to protect and empower the local trade sector, the society grew into a strong, community-focused organization. Today, it plays a vital role in the social and economic life of Adimali — supporting local businesses, fostering collaboration, and serving as a bridge between traders and local governance. Our journey continues with the same mission: strengthening the community through cooperation.
